Profile

Data-Miners is an independent research group in Universiti Teknologi MARA, Perlis branch. The niche area of Data-Miners is data mining using machine learning, statistical methods and mathematical modelling. The vision is to become a renowned RIG in the data science field. The mission is to gain more knowledge and techniques for analyzing data from different perspectives and summarize it into useful information. This RIG aims to provide a platform for knowledge advancement in data science, and train and produce postgraduates in data science.

Data-Miners had currently secured a few national research grants and supervised and nurtured postgraduates and undergraduates in the data science field. In the long run, Data-Miners is looking forward to university-industry collaboration to stimulate the technology transfer between universities and industry.
